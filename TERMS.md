## 2. Description of Service

Zeno is a self-custody browser extension wallet that enables users to:

- Store and manage cryptocurrency assets
- Send and receive tokens across multiple EVM-compatible blockchains
- Swap tokens via third-party DEX aggregators
- Purchase cryptocurrency via third-party fiat on-ramp providers (MoonPay)
- Interact with an AI assistant for wallet-related queries

## 3. Self-Custody — Your Responsibility

Zeno is a **non-custodial** wallet. This means:

- **We do not hold your funds.** Your assets exist on the blockchain, controlled solely by your private keys.
- **We cannot recover your wallet.** If you lose your seed phrase and password, your funds are permanently inaccessible. There is no recovery process.
- **You are responsible for securing your seed phrase.** Never share it with anyone, including Zeno support.
- **You are responsible for all transactions** you authorize. Blockchain transactions are irreversible.
